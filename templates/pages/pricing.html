Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "page"?

A page is any unique URL on your site. For example, /about, /contact, and /blog/my-post are each counted as one page. Your homepage (/) counts as one page too.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

We'll notify you before you hit your limits. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or pay a small overage fee. We'll never take your site offline without warning.

What does migration include?

We rebuild your site as optimized static HTML, compress and optimize all images, configure the CDN, set up your custom domain, and deploy everything. The result is a site that loads 7x faster with the same design you have now.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, your site stays live until the end of your billing period.

What if my site doesn't pass Core Web Vitals?

We guarantee all migrated sites pass Google's Core Web Vitals. If yours doesn't, we'll fix it at no extra cost. That's our speed guarantee.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service for any reason within the first 30 days, we'll refund your payment in full.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the credit will be applied to future invoices.

Do you offer custom or enterprise plans?

Yes! If you have specific requirements or need to host multiple sites, contact us and we'll create a custom plan that fits your needs.
